Featured Spots!

Intramuros (Manila)

Intramuros, "The Walled City," is the oldest district in Manila. This is what remains of the fortified city, which was the seat of power during Spanish Colonial times (1521-1898).

Rizal Park (Manila)

Historic park featuring a national hero's monument, tranquil gardens, and local culinary treats. Enjoy cultural exhibits and evening fountain displays in this serene urban retreat.

Manila Ocean Park (Manila)

Interactive marine adventure with underwater tunnel and educational exhibits on marine conservation. Highlights include penguin encounters and sea lion shows, set in a lively atmosphere.

Venice Grand Canal Mall (Taguig, City)

Venetian-themed mall with gondola rides along a scenic canal, surrounded by a blend of international dining and shopping outlets, ideal for romantic strolls and leisure.

Bonifacio Global City (Taguig, City)

Bonifacio Global City is the most posh suburb in Manila.

Art in Island (Quezon, City)

Prepare for magnificent sights of art and light crafted with the latest media technology, and a fully immersive experience that transcends time and space. Located in Quezon City, Art in Island

National Museum (Manila)

This grand exhibit house features the country's most historic works of culture and national heritage, including the paintings of heralded Philippine artist Felix Resurreccion Hidalgo.

Quezon Memorial Circle (Quezon, City)

The Quezon Memorial Circle grounds were drawn up by Arch. Francisco Mañosa and Associates. Some of the attractions are the Peace Bell, the Circle of Fun, the Children’s Playground, the Quezon Heritage House and many more.
